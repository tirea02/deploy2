Melody Tutorial

How to use Melody bot wise

Melody User's Guide: play command instructions

!!summon

Summons Melody to the voice channel.

!!play {youtube link}

Melody plays the video of the link you entered. If Melody is already playing something, plays the video at the end of the queue.

(ex) !!play https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EiVmQZwJhsA

!!play {search word}

Melody plays the video that searched by keyword you entered. If Melody is already playing something, plays the video at the end of the queue.

(ex) !!play IU-Friday

!!pause

Melody pauses playing and rests.

!!resume

Melody resumes playing.

!!stop

Melody stops playing and leaves from the voice channel.

!!skip

Melody stops playing and skips to next.

!!now

Melody shows the list of songs that is playing and is about to play.


Melody User's Guide: using playlist function

What is a 'playlist'? Melody remembers your playlist and helps you to enjoy your music easily:D

!!playlist create {playlist name}

Melody creates a new playlist with the name you entered.

(ex) !!playlist create my_music_list

!!playlist add {playlist name} {youtube link}

Melody saves the video link to the playlist.

(ex) !!playlist add my_music_list https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EiVmQZwJhsA

!!playlist show {playlist name}

Melody shows a list of videos stored in the playlist. And you can choose whether or not to play all the videos stored in the playlist.

(ex) !!playlist show my_music_list

!!playlist load {playlist name}

Melody is summoned to the voice channel and plays all the videos stored in the playlist.

(ex) !!playlist load my_music_list

!!playlist search {playlist name}

Melody searches playlist of other users. You can add searched playlist to current playing.

(ex) !!playlist search IUSongs

!!playlist delete {playlist name} {number}

Melody removes specific songs frome the playlist. The following example removes the second song in the iu playlist.

(ex) !!playlist delete iu 2

!!my

Melody shows list of playlist you owned.
